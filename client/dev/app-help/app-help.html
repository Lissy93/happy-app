What is Happy-App?

A simple web app to help gauge moral for members of the team. Data is collected anonymously, and results are displayed visually on the dashboard.

How does it work?

Automated emails are sent out to every team member at the same time each day, containing three links (good, average, bad). The user clicks a link, depending on how they feel the day went. Responses are then made anonymous and saved instantly.

Both team leads, and team members can see the dashboard, which shows a summary of results, for each team, and overall - for all teams. There are different charts to show different analytics, including sentiment over time, sentiment per team, and current sentiment breakdown.

The admin will be able to log in and configure tha apps settings, such as time and frequency of emails, and team member mailing lists.

How can I configure it for my team?

A server is required, and a copy of the files. A developer or system admin will then install it, which is a straightforward process. The team lead or SCRUM master will then configure the app and teams through the admin dashboard. This can later be modified at any time.
